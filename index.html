The Problem

A system in crisis. Children paying the price.

The UK SEND system is financially unsustainable, administratively overwhelmed, and producing plans that routinely fail legal standards.

£10.54B

Annual High Needs Spending

Spent each year on SEND provision with no structured evidence base showing which interventions work for which children.

Source: DfE High Needs Block 2024/25
53.6%

Plans Delivered Late

More than half of new EHCPs exceed the 20-week statutory deadline. Some councils achieve as low as 1% on-time delivery.

Source: DfE SEN2 2025 Release
£153M

Annual Tribunal Cost

22,276 tribunal cases registered in 2024 — a 43% surge. Councils lost 99% of the 11,157 cases that reached hearing.

Source: HMCTS Tribunal Statistics 2024; IPSEA
~£6B

Deficit Absorption

Government absorbing LA deficits — conditional on councils submitting approved reform plans demonstrating improved outcomes.

Source: DfE February 2026 Announcement

The Platform

From documentation to evidence

Sensol covers the full SEND framework — from ISPs for schools to EHCPs for local authorities — with outcomes tracking built in from day one.

1

Upload Professional Reports

EP reports, medical assessments, parental statements — the AI extracts and structures key findings automatically.

2

AI Generates Personalised Draft

RAG-powered drafting retrieves relevant guidance and case law without training on child data. Every statement is cited.

3

Quality Assurance & Golden Thread

Automated validation ensures needs link to outcomes link to provision. Vague or non-quantified statements are flagged.

4

Track Outcomes Over Time

Every annual review feeds structured data connecting provision to progress — building the evidence no one else collects.

Not faster paperwork. An evidence base.

Current tools make the existing broken process faster. Sensol asks a different question entirely: does the support specified in this plan actually improve the child's life?

Our platform builds the first structured, machine-readable dataset linking specific provisions to measured child outcomes across annual review cycles. Drawing on DfE attainment data, P-scales, attendance records, and the ECHILD linked dataset, we generate evidence that has never existed before.

What Sets Us Apart

Three capabilities no competitor offers

The market has validated that councils pay for EHCP automation. Sensol goes where existing tools stop.

📊

Longitudinal Outcomes Tracking

The only platform building a structured dataset linking SEND provision to measured child progress. No competitor addresses this — and the White Paper now makes outcomes accountability a condition of £6 billion in deficit funding.

Parent Transparency Portal

Every existing EHCP tool serves only council users. Sensol gives families real-time visibility into plan progress, plain-English explanations, and provision delivery status — breaking the information asymmetry that drives tribunal appeals.
